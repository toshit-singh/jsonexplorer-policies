Support

Last updated July 12, 2026

Need a hand with JSON Explorer? You’re in the right place. Below you’ll find how to reach us, what to include when something isn’t working, and answers to common questions.

1. Reporting a Bug

Found something broken? Tell us about it — the more detail, the faster we can fix it. Please include:

  • The app version (shown in Settings → About)
  • Your device model and operating system version
  • Step-by-step instructions to reproduce the issue
  • What you expected, and what happened instead
  • The JSON snippet or URL involved, if you’re comfortable sharing it

2. Feature Requests

Have an idea that would make JSON Explorer better? We’d love to hear it. Send a short note describing what you want and the problem it solves for you.

3. Managing Your Data

You can remove individual entries or clear all of your History at any time from the History screen. Uninstalling the app removes everything stored on your device — there is no server-side copy to delete, because your data never leaves your device in the first place.

4. Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where is my JSON stored?

    On your device only. Anything you paste, upload, or fetch is processed and saved locally. It is never sent to us or to any server we operate.

  • Can I fetch JSON from any URL?

    Yes — any URL your device can reach. The request goes directly from your device to the address you enter; we don’t proxy it. Plain http:// URLs may be blocked by your operating system for security, so prefer https://.

  • Do I need an account?

    No. There are no accounts, sign-ins, or subscriptions. The app works fully offline after install.

  • How do I switch between the list and tree views?

    Use the view toggle in the top bar while browsing, or set your default in Settings → Default view. Your choice is remembered.

  • Why did the app ask to read my clipboard?

    JSON Explorer checks the clipboard for valid JSON so it can offer a one-tap load. This happens only while the app is open, and the contents stay on your device unless you choose to load them.

5. Staying Up to Date

Keeping the app updated ensures you have the latest fixes and features. Updates are delivered through the App Store or Google Play. If you hit a persistent issue, confirming you’re on the latest version is a great first step.

6. Contact

For anything else, email toshit@tosnetic.com and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can.
