Privacy Policy

Last updated July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how JSON Explorer (“we”, “us”, or “the App”) handles information when you use the JSON Explorer mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information about you. JSON Explorer does not require an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, device identifiers, or location.

The App’s purpose is to let you view JSON that you paste, upload, or fetch from a URL. This data is processed and stored locally on your device and is never sent to us or to any server we operate.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

When you load JSON, the App may keep a local copy in your History so you can revisit it later. Your theme and default-view preferences are also saved. All of this is stored on your device using the operating system’s native storage and is never uploaded to us.

You are in control: you can delete individual entries or clear your entire History at any time from the History screen. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

3. Network Use

When you use the “Open URL” or Advanced API builder feature to fetch JSON, the App makes a network request directly from your device to the URL or API you specify. The App acts as a client on your behalf. We do not route this request through our servers, and we do not see, log, or store the request or its response.

Your use of any third-party URL or API is governed by that provider’s own terms and privacy practices. We encourage you to review them.

4. Clipboard Access

To make loading JSON faster, the App can read your device clipboard to detect whether you have copied JSON and offer to load it. Clipboard contents are read only while the App is open and only for this purpose; they are processed on-device.

Clipboard data is never transmitted off your device. It is added to your local History only if you choose to load the detected JSON.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not include any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, cross-app trackers, or other third-party services that collect data from you.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and developers. It is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Security

Because the App does not transmit personal information to us and stores your data locally on your device, the security of that data depends on the protections provided by your device’s operating system.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time, at our sole discretion, with or without prior notice.

When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date shown within the App. Your continued use of the App after a change becomes effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the updated policy, you should stop using the App.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this document or your use of JSON Explorer, you can contact Toshit Singh at toshit@tosnetic.com.

We will respond to privacy-related questions as soon as reasonably possible.
